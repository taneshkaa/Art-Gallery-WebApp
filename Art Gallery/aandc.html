Culture

Culture is a characteristics of a particular group of people, language, cousine, nation, music and art.
Culture is a customary belief - it can be passed or reflected through arts & crafts, cultural norm, cultural shows, etc.

Arts

Art is a kind of visual experience consciously created through an expression of skills and imaginations
There are great varieties of arts - Modern Art, Contemporary Art, Paintings, Historical Art, Textile Art, Pottery & Sculpture Art, Abstarct Art, etc.

Crafts

Craft is kind of an occupation requiring skills with hands or artistic styling, to reflect some cultural well beings.
There are great varieties of crafts - Paper Crafts, Houseware, Fiber & Textile Craft, Needlework, Ceramics Crafts, Fashion, etc.
